    How it works

    Colours blend the 45 scored Sydney suburbs through a 2.4 km gaussian, so pixels near multiple suburbs inherit a mix of their fit scores — that's why borders feather instead of cliff-edging. Each suburb's score is mean-centred cosine similarity between its feature vector and your quiz-derived taste vector, softly gated by your weekly budget.
